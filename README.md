# Primark Business Intelligence Project

## Overview
End-to-end data analytics project analysing Primark's business performance across four key areas: financial performance, gender pay parity, global store expansion, and sustainability progress.

## Tools Used
- **Python** — data extraction, cleaning, and loading (pdfplumber, pandas, mysql-connector)
- **MySQL** — relational database design and SQL analysis
- **Tableau** — interactive business intelligence dashboard

## Data Sources
- ABF Annual Reports 2019–2024 (revenue, profit, store count)
- UK Government Gender Pay Gap Service 2017–2025
- Primark Sustainability & Ethics Progress Reports 2021–2024

## Database Schema
Four tables: `primark_financials`, `primark_gender_pay`, `primark_sustainability`, `primark_store_expansion`

## Key Findings
- Revenue grew from £7.8bn (2019) to £9.4bn (2024) despite COVID-19 impact
- Mean gender pay gap reduced from 15.6% (2017) to 13.5% (2024)
- USA is Primark's fastest growing market with 22 new stores (2019–2024)
- Sustainable materials increased from 45% to 74% in just 3 years

## Dashboard
Built in Tableau Desktop with 4 KPI cards, 4 interactive charts, and 2 filters.
